The Dance of Light

A Story of Sunrise and Sunset

Golden rays paint the sleeping sky

The dawn awakens, whispers rise

Shadows retreat as the light grows bold

A canvas of warmth, a sight to behold

The horizon blushes in soft embrace

Morning unfolds with tender grace

Colors of fire, amber, and gold

Stories of dawn are gently retold

The sun ascends, breaking through clouds

Bathing the world in glowing shrouds

Life begins, the earth is alive

A rhythm of light helps all to survive

At twilight's edge, the sun now fades

A quiet peace, no rush, no haste

Crimson streaks stretch across the west

The day prepares for its final rest

The evening hums a lullaby sweet

Cool air descends, the night we meet

Shadows lengthen, the stars arrive

The world exhales, its glow survives
